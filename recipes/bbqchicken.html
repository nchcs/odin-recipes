BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

A quick and easy recipe, perfect for a weekday dinner!

These drumsticks are a weekday dinner staple, but can be upgraded if you have a bit of prep time.

  • 8 Chicken Drumsticks
  • 1 Tablespoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon of Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon of Black Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of Paprika
  • 4 Tablespoons of BBQ Sauce (Dealers Choice)
  • 8 Tablespoons of Salt
  • Cold Water
    •

    Steps

    1. Wash your chicken
    2. Preheat oven to 400 Degrees Farenheit
    3. Optional Soak chicken in cold water and salt for 12-24 hours
    4. Pat chicken dry and season with above spices
    5. Let Chicken bake 12.5 minutes on each side or until internal temp reaches 165 degrees Farenheit
    6. Brush or toss chicken with BBQ Sauce
    7. OptionalSet oven to broil and let chicken crisp up
    8. Enjoy
