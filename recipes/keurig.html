Keurig Coffee

A slow-roasted, hand-crafted, decadent, drink to start the day!

This toasty drink can be made with just a couple of button presses under a minute!

  • A Keurig Pod Of Your Choosing! Please ensure the pod you use is an official Keurig-brand pod, damage to the machine may occur if not.
  • A Coffee Mug
  • Water
    •

    Steps

    1. Wash your coffee mug
    2. Ensure Keurig machine has enough water in it
    3. Place OFFICIAL Keurig pod into the machine
    4. Hit the button
    5. Wait until machine has finished and coffee is cool enough to consume Not following this step may result in slight discomfort or death
    6. Enjoy!
